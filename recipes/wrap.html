WRAP

Wrap

Description

A Wrap is a culinary dish made with a soft flatbread rolled around a filling. The usual flatbreads are wheat rortillas, lavash or pita; the filling may include cold sliced meat, poultry or fish, shredded lettuce, diced tomato or pico de gallo among others.

  1. 2 (10 inch) flour tortillas
  2. ½ pound cooked pork roast, shredded
  3. ¼ cup barbeque sauce
  4. 4 slices Cheddar cheese, halved
  5. ½ small onion, thinly sliced into rings
  6. salt and ground black pepper to taste
